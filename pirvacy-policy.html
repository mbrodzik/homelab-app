Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This application ("Homelab Recipe Importer") is a personal-use tool that connects to Pinterest solely to read the owner's own saved pins and import recipe data into a private, self-hosted recipe manager.

Data Access

This app accesses Pinterest pin and board data belonging only to the app owner. No other users' data is accessed.

Data Storage

Recipe data is stored exclusively on a private home server. No data is transmitted to or stored on any third-party service or external server.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the app owner.
